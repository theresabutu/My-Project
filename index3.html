MY FAVOURITE MEAL

This page contains the recipe of my favorite meal


Chips and fried eggs

My favourite food is irish potato chips and egg.


A little about my favourite food (Chips and Eggs)

Egg and chips became popular in Britain during World War I due to a shortage of meat. It was a favourite food of the soldiers behind the lines on the Western Front in northern France and Belgium. Egg and chips is associated with a working-class diet. It has great health benefits, some of its Health benefits are: They are a great source of protein, Omega-3s and "good" cholesterol. They are a good source of vitamin B12, B6, vitamin D, selenium, and other important minerals!

Ingredients for Egg and Chips Recipe

Ingredient Measurement
1. Irish potato 450g (10-12 pcs)
2. Eggs 4
3. Onions 1pc
4. Carrots 3pcs
5. Fresh Pepper (Green, Red and yellow) 1 each
6. Vegetable oil for frying Just enough to have a deep fry
7. Seasoning salt and cubes 1 Tablespoon of salt and 2 cubes of desired seasoning
8. Tomato ketchup for garnishing 2 tablespoons
9. small garlic clove, minced and then mashed into a paste 1
10. Curry Powder 1 tablespoon

Method of Preparation

  1. Peel Irish potato, cut, wash, put in a drain basket to drain excess water, sprinkle some salt. Fry in hot vegetable oil until fried... I like my chips really dried so I fry it for a long time. Set aside!
  2. Break your eggs in a bowl, add onions,chopped carrot, fresh pepper, yellow, green pepper, mashed garlic, add a seasoning cube and or salt (I like using seasoning cubes to fry my eggs) add little curry powder, then whisk your egg to combine all ingredients evenly.
  3. Put a frying pan on fire,add some vegetable oil, allow to get hot, the pour your egg mixture. Fry on low heat, flip the other side of the egg to fry as well. Fry until golden brown.
  4. Your breakfast is ready! Serve the chips hot with the fried eggs and tomato ketchup. Enjoy!
